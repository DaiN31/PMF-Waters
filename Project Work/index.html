Our Mission

To make clean, affordable drinking water accessible to everyone. Through advanced purification technology, strict quality control, and dependable delivery, we ensure every sachet, bottle, and dispenser refill meets global safety standards.

Our Process: From Source to Sip

  1. Source: We draw from tested, deep underground aquifers and municipal sources that meet regulatory standards.
  2. Purify: 7-stage filtration system including sediment filtration, activated carbon, reverse osmosis, UV sterilization, and ozone treatment. This removes 99.9% of impurities, bacteria, and bad taste.
  3. Test: Daily in-house lab testing for pH, TDS, microbial safety, and taste. We’re NAFDAC registered and comply with SON/WHO guidelines.
  4. Package & Deliver: Automated, hygienic sealing in food-grade packaging. Then delivered fresh to homes, offices, schools, hotels, and events — anywhere we serve.

Our 5-Stage Purification Process

We use a multi-barrier system to ensure every sachet and bottle meets international drinking water standards. Clean, safe, and great-tasting water, every time.

1

Sediment Filtration

What it does: Removes sand, rust, silt, and other visible particles from raw water.

Why it matters: Protects downstream filters and gives you clear water with no grit.

2

Activated Carbon Filter

What it does: Absorbs chlorine, chemicals, and organic compounds that cause odor and taste.

Why it matters: Improves taste and removes the “tap water” smell.

3

RO Membrane

What it does: Forces water through a 0.0001-micron membrane to remove dissolved salts, heavy metals, and TDS.

Why it matters: Delivers ultra-pure water that meets NAFDAC and WHO standards.

4

UV Sterilization

What it does: Exposes water to UV-C light to neutralize bacteria, viruses, and other microbes.

Why it matters: Adds a chemical-free safety barrier against biological contaminants.

5

Ozonation + Polishing

What it does: Infuses ozone to kill any remaining microbes and extend shelf life. Final carbon polish improves taste.

Why it matters: Ensures water stays fresh, clean, and safe from bottling to your cup.
